## Arabic


<div dir="rtl" lang="ar" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
أَخْبَرَنَا قُتَيْبَةُ، عَنْ سُفْيَانَ، عَنْ عَبْدِ اللَّهِ بْنِ أَبِي بَكْرٍ، عَنْ عَبَّادِ بْنِ تَمِيمٍ، عَنْ عَمِّهِ، أَنَّ النَّبِيَّ صلى الله عليه وسلم اسْتَسْقَى وَصَلَّى رَكْعَتَيْنِ وَقَلَبَ رِدَاءَهُ ‏.‏
</div>

## English


<div dir="ltr" lang="en" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
It was narrated from 'Abbad bin Tamim, from his paternal uncle that:The Prophet (ﷺ) prayed for rain, and prayed two rak'ahs, and turned his rida' around

## Indonesian


<div dir="ltr" lang="id" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
