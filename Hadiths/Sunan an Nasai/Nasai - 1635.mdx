## Arabic


<div dir="rtl" lang="ar" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
أَخْبَرَنَا مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ عَبْدِ الأَعْلَى، قَالَ حَدَّثَنَا مُعْتَمِرٌ، عَنْ أَبِيهِ، عَنْ أَنَسٍ، أَنَّ النَّبِيَّ صلى الله عليه وسلم لَيْلَةَ أُسْرِيَ بِهِ مَرَّ عَلَى مُوسَى عَلَيْهِ السَّلاَمُ وَهُوَ يُصَلِّي فِي قَبْرِهِ ‏.‏
</div>

## English


<div dir="ltr" lang="en" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
It was narrated from Anas that:On the night on which he was taken on the Night Journey, the Prophet (ﷺ) passed by Musa, peace be upon him, and he was praying in his grave
</div>

## Indonesian


<div dir="ltr" lang="id" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
Telah mengabarkan kepada kami [Muhammad bin 'Abdul A'la] dia berkata; telah menceritakan kepada kami [Mu'tamir] dari [Bapaknya] dari [Anas] bahwa Rasulullah Shalallahu 'Alaihi Wa Sallam saat malam diisra'kan melewati Musa? laihis Salam, dan dia sedang shalat di kuburannya
</div>
