## Arabic


<div dir="rtl" lang="ar" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
أَخْبَرَنَا إِسْمَاعِيلُ بْنُ مَسْعُودٍ، حَدَّثَنَا خَالِدٌ، عَنْ شُعْبَةَ، عَنْ أَبِي إِسْحَاقَ، قَالَ سَمِعْتُ أَبَا سَلَمَةَ، عَنْ أُمِّ سَلَمَةَ، قَالَتْ مَا مَاتَ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ صلى الله عليه وسلم حَتَّى كَانَ أَكْثَرُ صَلاَتِهِ قَاعِدًا إِلاَّ الْفَرِيضَةَ وَكَانَ أَحَبُّ الْعَمَلِ إِلَيْهِ أَدْوَمَهُ وَإِنْ قَلَّ ‏.‏
</div>

## English


<div dir="ltr" lang="en" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
It was narrated from Abu Salamah, that Umm Salamah said:"The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) did not pass away until most of his prayers were offered sitting down, except for the obligatory prayers, and the dearest of actions to him were those which were done persistently, even if they were few
</div>

## Indonesian


<div dir="ltr" lang="id" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
Telah mengabarkan kepada kami [Isma'il bin Mas'ud] telah menceritakan kepada kami [Khalid] dari [Syu'bah] dari [Abu Ishaq] dia berkata; Aku mendengar [Abu Salamah] dari [Ummu Salamah] dia berkata; "Tidaklah Rasulullah shallallahu 'alaihi wasallam wafat hingga kebanyakan shalatnya adalah sambil duduk, kecuali shalat fardlu. Perbuatan yang paling dicintai adalah yang dilakukan secara kontinyu, walaupun sedikit
</div>

## Urdu


<div dir="rtl" lang="ur" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
