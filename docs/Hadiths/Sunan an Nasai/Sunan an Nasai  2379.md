## Arabic


<div dir="rtl" lang="ar" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
أَخْبَرَنَا عَلِيُّ بْنُ حُجْرٍ، قَالَ أَنْبَأَنَا إِسْمَاعِيلُ، عَنِ الْجُرَيْرِيِّ، عَنْ يَزِيدَ بْنِ عَبْدِ اللَّهِ بْنِ الشِّخِّيرِ، عَنْ أَخِيهِ، مُطَرِّفٍ عَنْ عِمْرَانَ، قَالَ قِيلَ يَا رَسُولَ اللَّهِ إِنَّ فُلاَنًا لاَ يُفْطِرُ نَهَارًا الدَّهْرَ ‏.‏ قَالَ ‏ "‏ لاَ صَامَ وَلاَ أَفْطَرَ ‏"‏ ‏.‏
</div>

## English


<div dir="ltr" lang="en" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
It was narrated that 'Imran said:"It was said: 'O Messenger of Allah, so and so never broke his fast, even for one day for the rest of his life.' He said: 'He has neither fasted nor broken his fast."" 'Ata said: "someone who heard him told me that Ibn 'Umar (said) that the Prophet said: 'Whoever fasts every day of his life, then he has not fasted
</div>

## Indonesian


<div dir="ltr" lang="id" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
Telah mengabarkan kepada kami ['Ali bin Hujr] dia berkata; telah memberitakan kepada kami [Isma'il] dari [Al Jurairi] dari [Yazid bin 'Abdullah bin Asy Syakhkhir] dari saudaranya [Mutharrif] dari ['Imran] dia berkata; seseorang bertanya; "Wahai Rasulullah shallallahu 'alaihi wasallam, si fulan tidak berbuka di siang hari terus-menerus sepanjang masa?" Beliau bersabda: "Ia -dianggap- tidak berpuasa dan tidak berbuka
</div>

## Urdu


<div dir="rtl" lang="ur" style={{fontSize:'larger',backgroundColor:'#f8f9fa',padding:20}}>
